About Me

Bryan Dunleavy is a dedicated REALTOR® with eXp Realty serving Greenville and the Western Upstate of South Carolina. With 9 years of experience and a natural passion for real estate, he provides honest, reliable service and deep local market knowledge to help clients find their happy place.

Bryan is also an active community leader. He serves as Vice President of the Greenville St. Patrick's Day Parade & Festival Committee and President of the Ancient Order of Hibernians – Harp & Shamrock Division. He regularly supports St. Baldrick's Foundation through head-shaving events for childhood cancer research.

A proud Greenville resident, Bryan is committed to both professional excellence and giving back to the community he calls home.
